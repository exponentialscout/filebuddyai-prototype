Representation Agreement
🔴 Required Before Broker Review
Why this is required
Before the broker can review the purchase agreement, a signed representation agreement must be on file. This confirms the agent-client relationship and is required by CA law (AB 2992, effective Jan 1, 2025) and brokerage policy for all transactions.
What to upload
For buyers: Buyer Representation and Broker Compensation Agreement (BRBC / BRA)

For sellers: Residential Listing Agreement (RLA) or Exclusive Authorization and Right to Sell
Broker alert
If no representation agreement is confirmed when the broker opens this transaction for review, they will see a blocking alert and cannot proceed without either getting the agreement or manually overriding (which is logged in the audit trail).
